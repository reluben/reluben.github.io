Disc Golf for Beginners

Throw discs into baskets in as few throws as possible. Free courses in parks. Just show up.

Rules You Need Day 1

  • Farthest from basket throws next (“away”)
  • Play it where it lies
  • Disc must finish in basket/chains
  • Mark lie with mini or leave disc & stand behind
  • Out-of-bounds = +1 stroke & re-throw (or from last in-bounds)
  • Don’t hit people

Start with 3 Discs (150–170g)

Innova Starter Set Aviar Shark Leopard

This is a good starter set, all in DX plastic.
Different plastics that affect how discs fly and feel to you. If you like the Leopard, try it in Star plastic instead of DX.
Additionally, discs “break-in” and change flight as they get used (and hit trees). Harder plastics resist this more and take longer to change their flight.

Fastest Way to Suck Less

  1. Play 10 rounds (either 9 or 18 holes) with a putter only
  2. Throw 100 discs in a field (flat & nose-down)
  3. Putt from 7–10 m every round
  4. Film yourself
