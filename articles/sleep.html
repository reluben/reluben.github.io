Improving Your Sleep

  • Get sunlight first thing in the morning insha’allah
  • At night use candle light and/or a red light filter on every light source
  • Sleep in a room that is 18.3°C (65°F) and 45%-55% relative humidity
  • Wear wool socks if your feet are cold (helps fall asleep faster)
  • Keep a journal next to your bed for brain dump
  • Have many plants in your house (and bedroom at night) to breathe fresh air
  • Sleep naked for better temperature regulation, especially for men due to their scrotum
Some additional things that have helped me since hurting my back not mentioned above:
  • Linen sheets
  • Silk-filled comforter (never overheat)
  • Firm latex mattress (I use a topper by SleepOnLatex, they claim it is an enhanced Dunlop)
  • 900+ fill-power down pillow
  • Wool pillow (they have an adjustable fill)
  • Silk pillowcase (this is the least effective one I believe)

There are two of latex types: talalay and dunlop. Latex is made from the sap of the rubber tree. It is anti-microbial and doesn't smell weird the way plastic made of petroleum does. The sap is mixed with water to form latex which is whipped into a frothy foam.

Talalay Dunlop
Mold is partially filled with latex, vacuum-sealed to expand the latex, and then flash-frozen, washed, and dried. Mold is fully filled with latex, baked, washed, and dried.
