DISC GOLF IN 5 MINUTES

Throw discs into baskets in as few throws as possible. Free courses in parks. Just show up.

RULES YOU NEED DAY 1

  • Farthest from basket throws next ("away")
  • Play it where it lies
  • Disc must finish in basket/chains
  • Mark lie with mini or leave disc & stand behind
  • Elevated >2 m? Mark directly below, no penalty
  • OB = +1 stroke & re-throw or from last in-bounds
  • Don’t hit people

START WITH 3 DISCS (150g-170g)

All discs below are by Innova

FASTEST WAY TO SUCK LESS

  1. Play 10 rounds with ONE mid or putter only
  2. Throw 100 discs in a field (flat & nose-down)
  3. Putt from 7-10 m every round
  4. Learn hyzer / flat / anhyzer
  5. Film yourself
